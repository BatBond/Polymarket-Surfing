HOW THE BRAIN DECIDES

For each market, the brain computes:
• Momentum: recent price direction
• Mean reversion: distance from 20-period MA
• Bayesian edge: posterior probability vs market price
• Volume score: normalized 0-1

Composite signal = weighted sum. If signal > threshold AND confidence ≥ min, the market becomes a candidate. Top candidate goes through risk gates.

RISK GATES (ALL must pass)

1. Budget: cash_available ≥ order_notional
2. Slots: open_positions < max_concurrent
3. Cap: $30 ≤ notional ≤ $40 (server-enforced)
4. No duplicate: not already in this market
5. Drawdown: current_dd < 20%
6. Confidence: signal_confidence ≥ min_threshold
